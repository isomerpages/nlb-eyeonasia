<p>President Xi Jinping’s 2024 New Year message to China was optimistic,
despite some economic setbacks in 2023. As we move into the new year, enterprising
businesses and individuals have much to benefit from exploring new opportunities
in this recovering region. Hear from experts and business practitioners
and gain insights into what the Chinese market can offer, and how Singapore
can tap into China’s new directions.</p>

<h3><strong>About the Speakers</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Dr Victor Tay</strong> 
<br><strong>CEO of Global Catalyst Advisory</strong> 
<br><strong>Governing Council and CEO of China ASEAN Business Alliance</strong>
</p>
<p>Victor Tay is the Chief Executive Office at Global Catalyst Advisory and
also the Managing Director of Stout Asia, Investment Banking group managing
on South East Asia office. He is also a General Partner with Da One Global
Ventures which focuses on transforming technologies industry. Concurrently
he sits at the Governing Council and acts as the CEO of China ASEAN Business
Alliance.
<br>
<br>His areas of expertise include corporate strategic development, market
entry and expansion strategy, direct investment, M&amp;A and corporate
finance advisory, and transaction support. He has assisted a number of
Asian companies in going public successfully.
<br>
<br>Previously Victor was the acting CEO and COO of Singapore Business Federation,
when he represents and leads Singapore delegation to Nanning, Guangxi which
the China ASEAN Business Forum is hosted. Victor was appointed as Singapore
representative to ASEAN Young Business Leader platform. Concurrently he
was also the chairman of United Nations Global Company NS.
<br>
<br>Victor was a scholarship recipient with University of Oxford and had studied
at Harvard University.</p>
<p><strong>Lim Tern Poh</strong> 
<br><strong>ASEAN-China Young Leaders Scholar</strong>
</p>
<p>Lim Tern Poh (TP) is an aspiring global leader. He seeks to bridge Western
and Eastern thinking to facilitate international collaborations and create
business opportunities. He is pursuing his dual-degree MBA at Guanghua
School of Management, Peking University, and the National University of
Singapore under the prestigious ASEAN-China Young Leaders Scholarship awarded
by the Chinese government.</p>
<p>Before his MBA, his experience included Associate Director at AI Singapore
and Head of AI Product at AI2Lab. He holds a Master of Computing (AI) from
NUS. TP’s interdisciplinary experience allows him to combine AI technical
expertise with strategic foresight to create impact at scale.</p>
<p><strong>Tan Hong Lye, Cecilia</strong> 
<br><strong>CEO of Sasseur Asset Management Pte. Ltd.</strong>
</p>
<p>Cecilia was appointed the CEO of Sasseur Asset Management Pte Ltd in August
2021. She brings with her more than 20 years of professional career experience
spanning across a wide spectrum of real estate expertise in real estate
investment banking, real estate direct investment, REIT management, real
estate fund management and property development.</p>
<p>Before joining Sasseur Asset Management, Ms Tan was the Senior Strategic
Adviser to Mr Vito Xu, the Chairman and Founder of Sasseur Group, the Sponsor
of Sasseur REIT. Her previous appointments included being the Chief Financial
Officer and Chief Operating Officer of KOP Group Pte Ltd, Executive Vice-President
(Real Estate Fund Management) of Pacific Star Group, and Chief Investment
Officer of Macquarie Pacific Star Prime REIT Management Pte Ltd, the manager
of Macquarie MEAG Prime REIT (now known as Starhill Global REIT) and an
Independent Director of Roxy-Pacific Holdings Limited</p>
